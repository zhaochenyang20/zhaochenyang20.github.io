微积分WXF第二章作业 作者：鲁睿

习题2.1

4(3)

解：先证明 \(Poission\) 积分 \(\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx({=\dfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}})\) 收敛

引理：\(\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx\) 收敛，代换 \(t=x^2\)，有 \(\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx=\dfrac{1}{2}(\int_0^1\dfrac{e^{-t}}{\sqrt{t}}dt+\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{e^{-t}}{\sqrt{t}}dt)\)

前者 \(\displaystyle\int_0^1\dfrac{e^{-t}}{\sqrt{t}}dt<\int_0^1\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{t}}dt=2\)，后者 \(\displaystyle\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{e^{-t}}{\sqrt{t}}dt<\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-t}dt=\dfrac{1}{e}\)

显然 \(f(A)=\displaystyle\int_0^{A}\dfrac{e^{-t}}{\sqrt{t}}dt\) 单调递增，且有上界 \(2+\dfrac{1}{e}\)，从而 \(\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx\) 收敛

放缩有 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}x^{2n}e^{-tx^2}dx\leq\int_0^{+\infin}x^{2n}e^{-t_0x^2}dx\)，只需要考虑后者的收敛性（比较判别法）

由极限 \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infin}x^{2n}e^{-\frac{1}{2}t_0x^2}=0\)，且有 \(x^{2n}e^{-\frac{1}{2}tx^2\big |_{x=0}}=0\)，则由连续性知令 \(\max\{x^{2n}e^{-\frac{1}{2}t_0x^2}\}=M\) \[ \forall\ \epsilon>0,\exist\ M>0,\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}x^{2n}e^{-t_0x^2}dx\leq M\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-\frac{1}{2}t_0x^2}dx\leq M\sqrt{\dfrac{2}{t_0}}\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-m^2}dm\\ \] 由单调性和上界知 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}x^{2n}e^{-t_0x^2}dx\) 收敛，则 \(\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}x^{2n}e^{-\frac{1}{2}tx^2}dx\) 在 \(t\in[t_0,+\infin)\) 一致收敛

4(5)

解：由被积函数为偶函数得 \(f(t)=\displaystyle\int_{-\infin}^{+\infin}\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}dx=2\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}dx\)，其中 \[ \because\forall\ x\in[0,1],\dfrac{x^2}{1+x^4}=\dfrac{1}{x^2+\dfrac{1}{x^2}}\leq\dfrac{1}{2},\forall\ x\in R,|\cos(tx)|\leq1\\ \therefore|\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}dx|\leq\int_0^{+\infin}|\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}|dx=\int_0^1|\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}|dx+\int_1^{+\infin}|\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}|dx\\ \leq \dfrac{1}{2}\int_0^11\cdot dx+\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{x^2}{1+x^4}dx=\dfrac{1}{2}+\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{1}{x^2}dx=\dfrac{3}{2} \] 则 \(\displaystyle\int_{-\infin}^{+\infin}\abs{\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}}dx\leq3\) 收敛，则 \(\displaystyle\int_{-\infin}^{+\infin}\dfrac{x^2\cos(tx)}{1+x^4}dx\) 对 \(t\in R\) 一致收敛

4(7)

解：对 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx\) 取绝对值拆为两部分放缩，对 \(t\geq 0\) 时总有从而收敛 \[ \displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx=\int_0^1e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx+\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx\leq\displaystyle \int_0^11\cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}}dx+\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx\\ \] 前者为 \(2\sqrt{x}\big |_0^{1}=2\)，后者分部积分放缩得 \[ I(t)=\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{d(\sin x)}{\sqrt{x}}=\dfrac{e^{-tx}\sin x}{\sqrt{x}}\big |_1^{+\infin}+\int_1^{+\infin}\sin x\cdot \dfrac{-te^{-tx}\cdot \sqrt{x}-e^{-tx}\cdot \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}dx\\ =-e^{-t}\sin 1-\dfrac{1}{2}\int_1^{+\infin}(\dfrac{e^{-tx}\sin x}{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}+2t\cdot \dfrac{e^{-tx}\sin x}{\sqrt{x}})dx<0+\dfrac{1}{2}\int_1^{+\infin}(\dfrac{dx}{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}+2t\cdot \dfrac{e^{-tx}d(\cos x)}{\sqrt{x}})\\ =e^{-t}(-\dfrac{2}{\sqrt{x}})\big |_1^{+\infin}+t\cdot \dfrac{e^{-tx}\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}\big |_1^{+\infin}+t\int_1^{+\infin}\cos x\cdot \dfrac{-te^{-tx}\cdot \sqrt{x}-e^{-tx}\cdot \dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}}{x}dx\\ \leq2e^{-t}-te^{-t}\cos 1+\dfrac{t}{2}\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{e^{-t}\cdot 1}{x^{\frac{3}{2}}}dx-t^2\cdot\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx\leq2-te^{-t}\cos 1+\dfrac{t}{2}\cdot 2-t^2I(t)\\ I(t)<\dfrac{2+(1-\cos 1)\cdot t}{1+t^2}=g(t)\ \because g(0)=2,\lim\limits_{t\to +\infin}g(t)=0\ \therefore\exist \ M>0,\forall \ t\geq 0,g(t)\leq M \] 从而 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}dx\) 有常数上限 \(2+M\)，则 \(I(t)\) 对 \(t\geq0\) 均一致收敛

4(10)

解：对该积分换元 \(t=x^2\) ，有 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin x^2}{x^{p}}dx=\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin t}{t^{\frac{p}{2}}}\cdot \dfrac{1}{2}t^{-\frac{1}{2}}dt=\dfrac{1}{2}\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin t}{t^{\frac{p+1}{2}}}dt\)

由极限 \(\lim\limits_{t\to 0}\dfrac{\sin x}{x}=1\)，有 \(\exist\ \dfrac{\pi}{2}>x_0>0,\forall \abs{x}<x_0,s.t.\dfrac{\sin x}{x}\geq\dfrac{1}{2}\)，则 \(\dfrac{\sin t}{t^{\frac{p+1}{2}}}=\dfrac{\sin t}{t}\cdot t^{\frac{1-p}{2}}\geq\dfrac{1}{2}t^{\frac{1-p}{2}}\)

若 \(p\geq3\)，有 \(\displaystyle \int_{0}^{x_0}\dfrac{\sin t}{t^{\frac{p+1}{2}}}dt\geq\dfrac{1}{2}\int_0^{x_0}\dfrac{dt}{t^{\frac{p-1}{2}}}\) 积分发散，从而该函数并非一致收敛

5

证明：\(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx=\int_0^1e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx+\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx\leq3+\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx\\\)

在 \([0,1]\) 区间上积分有常数上限，进而该区间一致收敛，只需考虑 \([1,+\infin)\) 区间，当 \(t=0\) 时 \[ \displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx=\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx=\int_0^1\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx+\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx\le3+\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx\\ \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx=-\dfrac{1}{3}\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{d(\cos 3x)}{x}=-\dfrac{1}{3}\dfrac{\cos 3x}{x}\big |_1^{+\infin}+\frac{1}{3}\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\cos 3x}{x^2}dx\\ \because \int_1^{+\infin}\abs{\dfrac{\cos 3x}{x^2}}dx\leq\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{dx}{x^2}=1\ \therefore\int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\cos 3x}{x^2}dx\ 收敛\ \therefore \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx \ 收敛 \] 从而 \(t=0\) 时收敛且 \(\displaystyle\int_1^{+\infin}f(x)dx= \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x}dx\) 收敛，考虑 \(g(x)=\dfrac{x}{x+t}e^{-tx}\ (t>0)\) ，其导数为 \[ \dfrac{dg(x)}{dx}=\dfrac{(e^{-tx}-txe^{-tx})\cdot (x+t)-xe^{-tx}\cdot 1}{(x+t)^2}=\dfrac{t(1-(x+t)x)}{(x+t)^2}e^{-tx}\\ \because x\geq1,\ t>0\therefore 1-(x+t)x<0,\ \dfrac{dg(x)}{dx}<0\therefore g(x)单调递减,\ 0<g(x)\leq g(1)=\dfrac{e^{-t}}{t+1} \] 由 \(Abel\) 判别法，\(\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}e^{-tx}\dfrac{\sin 3x}{x+t}dx=\int_1^{+\infin}f(x)g(x)dx\)，\(f(x)\) 收敛，\(g(x)\) 单调有界，原函数一致收敛

8

证明：\(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin tx}{x}dx\) 对 \(\forall \ t>0\) 换元 \(tx=y\in[0,+\infin)\) 有 \[ \displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin tx}{x}dx=\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{t\sin y}{y}\cdot \dfrac{1}{t}dy=\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin y}{y}dy=\dfrac{\pi}{2} \] 而 \(\forall \ t<0\)，换元 \(tx=y\in(-\infin,0]\) 有 \[ \displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin tx}{x}dx=\int_0^{-\infin}\dfrac{t\sin y}{y}\cdot \dfrac{1}{t}dy=\int_0^{-\infin}\dfrac{\sin y}{y}dy=-\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\sin y}{y}dy=-\dfrac{\pi}{2} \] 从而只要区间包含 \(t=0\) 则在 \(t=0\) 处不连续

2(2)

解：对 \(F(y)=\displaystyle\int_{a+y}^{b+y}\dfrac{sin(yx)}{x}dx\)，其对 \(x\) 的导函数分为两部分，分类讨论 \[ F'(y)=\dfrac{sin(y(b+y))}{b+y}-\dfrac{sin(y(a+y))}{a+y}+\int_{a+y}^{b+y}sin(xy)dx\\=(\dfrac{1}{b+y}+\dfrac{1}{y})sin(y(b+y))-(\dfrac{1}{a+y}+\dfrac{1}{y})sin(y(a+y)) \] 其中 \(y=0\) 时，代入求极限得 \(F'(y)=b-a\) ；

当 \(y=-b\) 时，代入求极限得 \(F'(y)=-b+(\dfrac{1}{b-a}+\dfrac{1}{b})sin(b(b-a))\)；

当 \(y=-a\) 时，代入求极限得 \(F'(y)=(\dfrac{1}{b-a}-\dfrac{1}{a})sin(a(a-b))+a\)

2(3)

解：对 \(F(t)=\displaystyle\int_0^{t}\dfrac{\ln(1+tx)}{x}dx\)，其对 \(t\) 求导得 \[ F'(t)=\dfrac{\ln(1+t^2)}{t}+\int_0^{t}\dfrac{1}{1+tx}dx=\dfrac{\ln(1+t^2)}{t}+\dfrac{1}{t}\ln(1+tx)\big |_0^t=\dfrac{2\ln(1+t^2)}{t} \] 当 \(t=0\) 时，代入求极限得 \(F'(t)=0\)

解：由提示变换 \(\dfrac{\arctan x}{x}=\displaystyle\int_0^1\dfrac{1}{1+x^2y^2}dy\)，化为二重积分得 \[ \int_0^1\dfrac{\arctan x}{x}\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx=\int_0^1\displaystyle\int_0^1\dfrac{1}{1+x^2y^2}\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dxdy \] 代换 \(x=\sin\varphi,\varphi\in[0,\dfrac{\pi}{2}]\)，有 \(\dfrac{1}{1+x^2y^2}\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx=\dfrac{d\varphi}{1+y^2\sin^2\varphi}=\dfrac{\cos^2\varphi \cdot d(\tan \varphi)}{(1+y^2)\sin^2\varphi+\cos^2 \varphi}\) \[ \int_0^1\dfrac{\arctan x}{x}\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx=\int_0^1(\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{d(\tan \varphi)}{1+(1+y^2)\tan^2\varphi})dy=\int_0^1(\dfrac{\arctan (\sqrt{1+y^2}\tan \varphi)}{\sqrt{1+y^2}})\big |_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}dy\\ =\dfrac{\pi}{2}\int_0^1\dfrac{dy}{\sqrt{1+y^2}}=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln(y+\sqrt{1+y^2})\big |_0^1=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln(1+\sqrt{2}) \]

5(2)

解：由 \(\dfrac{x^b-x^a}{\ln x}=\displaystyle \int_a^bx^ydy\)，化为二重积分得 \[ \int_0^1\dfrac{x^b-x^a}{\ln x}\sin(\ln\dfrac{1}{x})dx=-\int_0^1\int_a^bx^y\sin(\ln x)dxdy=-\int_a^bdy\int_0^1x^y\sin(\ln x)dx \] 变换 \(t=\ln x,t\in(-\infin,0]\)，有 \(x^y\sin(\ln x)dx=e^{yt}\sin t\cdot e^tdt=e^{(y+1)t}\sin tdt\)，分部积分得 \[ \int_0^1x^y\sin(\ln x)dx=\int_{-\infin}^0e^{(y+1)t}\sin tdt=-\cos t\cdot e^{(y+1)t}\big |_{-\infin}^0+(y+1)\int_{-\infin}^0\cos t\cdot e^{(y+1)t}dt\\ =-1+(y+1)\int_{-\infin}^0e^{(y+1)t}d(\sin t)=-1+(y+1)(e^{(y+1)t}\sin t\big |_{-\infin}^0-(y+1)\int_{-\infin}^0e^{(y+1)t}\sin tdt)\\ =-1-(y+1)^2\int_{-\infin}^0e^{(y+1)t}\sin tdt \Longrightarrow\int_{-\infin}^0e^{(y+1)t}\sin tdt=-\dfrac{1}{1+(y+1)^2} \] 代入原式得 \[ \int_0^1\dfrac{x^b-x^a}{\ln x}\sin(\ln\dfrac{1}{x})dx=\int_a^b\dfrac{dy}{1+(y+1)^2}=\arctan (1+y)\big |_a^b=\arctan (1+b)-\arctan (1+a) \]

习题2.3

1(1)

解：\(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{e^{-ax^2}-e^{-bx^2}}{x}dx\ (a,b>0)=\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{1}{x}dx\int_a^bx^2e^{-x^2y}dy=\int_0^{+\infin}\int_a^bxe^{-x^2y}dxdy\) \[ \int_0^{+\infin}xe^{-x^2y}dx=\dfrac{1}{2}\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2y}d(x^2)=-\dfrac{1}{2y}\cdot e^{-x^2y}\big |_0^{+\infin}=\dfrac{1}{2y}\\ \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{e^{-ax^2}-e^{-bx^2}}{x}dx=\int_a^{b}\dfrac{1}{2y}dy=\dfrac{1}{2}\ln (\dfrac{b}{a}) \]

1(2)

解：由定理 \(2.3.2\) 对 \(x\) 的广义积分和对 \(y\) 的导数运算可以交换次序得 \[ \displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}xe^{-ax^2}\sin(yx)dx\ (a>0)=-\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{\part (\cos (yx)e^{-ax^2})}{\part y}dx=-\dfrac{d}{dy}(\int_0^{+\infin}\cos (yx)e^{-ax^2}dx) \]

引理：教材例 \(2.3.2\) 给出 \(I(y)=\displaystyle\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-ax^2}\cos (xy)dx=\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{\dfrac{\pi}{a}}e^{-\frac{y^2}{4a}}\) ，使用分部积分证明： \[ \dfrac{dI}{dy}=I'(y)=-\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}xe^{-ax^2}\sin(xy)dx=\dfrac{1}{2a}\int_0^{+\infin}\sin(xy)d(e^{-ax^2})\\=\dfrac{1}{2a}(\sin(xy)e^{-ax^2}\big |_0^{+\infin}-\int_0^{+\infin}y\cos(xy)e^{-ax^2}dx)=-\dfrac{y}{2a}\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-ax^2}\cos(xy)dx=-\dfrac{y}{2a}I(y) \] 积分得 \(\ln I=-\dfrac{y^2}{4a}+C\)，代入 \(Poission\) 积分 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-x^2}dx=\dfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}\)，有 \[ I(0)=\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}e^{-ax^2}dx=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\int_0^{+\infin}e^{-t^2}dt=\dfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2\sqrt{a}}\Longrightarrow C=\ln (\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{\dfrac{\pi}{a}}),I(y)=\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{\dfrac{\pi}{a}}e^{-\frac{y^2}{4a}} \]

则 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}xe^{-ax^2}\sin(yx)dx=-\dfrac{d}{dy}(\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{\dfrac{\pi}{a}}e^{-\frac{y^2}{4a}})=\dfrac{y}{4a}\sqrt{\dfrac{\pi}{a}}e^{-\frac{y^2}{4a}}\)

第2章复习题

4(1)

解：\(I(a,b)=\displaystyle \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (a^2\sin ^2x+b^2\cos^2 x)dx\) 由 \(I(a,b)=I(\abs{a},\abs{b})\)，只需考虑 \(a,b\geq0\) 的情况 \[ \dfrac{\part I}{\part a}=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{2a\sin ^2x}{a^2\sin ^2x+b^2\cos^2 x}dx=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{2a\sin^2x\cdot d(\tan x)}{a^2\tan^2x+b^2}=2a\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\tan^2x}{1+\tan^2x}\dfrac{d(\tan x)}{a^2\tan^2x+b^2}\\ t=\tan x\in[0,+\infin),\dfrac{\part I}{\part a}=2a\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{t^2dt}{(1+t^2)(a^2t^2+b^2)}=2a\int_0^{+\infin}(\dfrac{Adt}{1+t^2}+\dfrac{Bdt}{a^2t^2+b^2})\\ \] 当 \(a\neq b\) 时，有对应系数相等 \(\begin{cases}Aa^2+B=1\\Ab^2+B=0\end{cases}\)，解得 \(\begin{cases}A=\dfrac{1}{a^2-b^2}\\B=-\dfrac{b^2}{a^2-b^2}\end{cases}\)，则代入有 \[ \because \displaystyle \int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{dt}{1+t^2}=\arctan t\big |_0^{+\infin}=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ \ ,\int_0^{+\infin}\dfrac{dt}{a^2t^2+b^2}=\dfrac{1}{ab}\arctan(\dfrac{at}{b})\big |_0^{+\infin}=\dfrac{\pi}{2ab}\\ \therefore \dfrac{\part I}{\part a}=2a(\dfrac{1}{a^2-b^2}\cdot \dfrac{\pi}{2}-\dfrac{b^2}{a^2-b^2}\cdot \dfrac{\pi}{2ab})=\dfrac{\pi}{a+b},I(a)=\pi\ln(a+b)+C \] 而当 \(a=b\) 时，\(\dfrac{\part I}{\part a}=\displaystyle2a\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2 xdx}{a^2(\sin^2x+\cos^2x)}=\dfrac{2}{a}\cdot \dfrac{\pi}{4}=\dfrac{\pi}{2a}\)，也与上述结果相同，代入 \(a=0\)

\(Euler\) 积分： \(\displaystyle\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (\sin x)dx=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (\cos x)dx=-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln 2\)，考虑 \(t=\dfrac{1}{2}x\in[0,\dfrac{\pi}{4}]\) \[ \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln(\sin x)dx=2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\ln(\sin 2t)dt=2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}(\ln2+\ln(\cos t)+\ln(\sin t))dx\\ =\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln 2+2\cdot (\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\ln (\sin t )dt+\int_{\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\ln (\cos (\dfrac{\pi}{2}-t')))\cdot (-dt')=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln 2+2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (\sin x)dx\\ -\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln(\sin x)dx=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln 2\Longrightarrow \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (\sin x)dx=-\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln 2 \]

\[ I(0,b)=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (b^2\cos ^2x)dx=\pi\ln b+2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln (\cos x)dx=\pi\ln (\dfrac{b}{2}) \]

代入 \(I(a,b)=\pi \ln(a+b)+C\)，得 \(C=-\pi \ln 2\)，即 \(a,b\geq0,\ I(a,b)=\pi\ln(\dfrac{a+b}{2})\)

从而有当 \(a=b=0\) 时原积分不存在，当 \(a,b\) 不同为 \(0\) 时， \(I(a,b)=\pi\ln (\dfrac{\abs{a}+\abs{b}}{2})\)

4(2)

解：\(I(a)=\displaystyle \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\arctan(a\tan x)}{\tan x}dx\)，对参数 \(a\) 求导得 \(I'(a)=\displaystyle \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\tan x}{(1+a^2\tan ^2x)\tan x}dx\)

由上小问知 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{2a\sin ^2x}{a^2\sin ^2x+b^2\cos^2 x}dx=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{2a\cos ^2x}{a^2\cos ^2x+b^2\sin^2 x}dx=\dfrac{\pi}{a+b}\)，代入有 \[ \dfrac{dI}{da}=I'(a)=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\cos ^2x}{a^2\sin ^2x+\cos ^2x}dx=\dfrac{\pi}{a+1}\cdot \dfrac{1}{2}=\dfrac{\pi}{2(a+1)} \] 积分得 \(I(a)=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln (a+1)+C\)，显然 \(I(0)=\displaystyle \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}0\cdot dx=0\)，得 \(C=0\) ，即 \(I(a)=\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln (a+1)\)

考虑 \(a\) 的符号有 \(I(a)=sgn(a)\dfrac{\pi}{2}\ln (\abs{a}+1),where\ sgn(x)=\begin{cases}1,x>0\\0,x=0\\-1,x<0\end{cases}\)

5(1)

解：\(\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}=\lim\limits_{A\to +\infin}\int_1^A\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}=\lim\limits_{A\to +\infin}\dfrac{x}{x^2+y^2}\big |_1^{A}=\lim\limits_{A\to +\infin}\dfrac{A}{A^2+y^2}-\dfrac{1}{1+y^2}\) \[ \forall \ \epsilon>0,\exist \ \delta>\dfrac{1}{\epsilon},\forall \ A>\delta,s.t.\dfrac{A}{A^2+y^2}\leq\dfrac{1}{A}<\epsilon \] 从而 \(f(y)=\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}=0-\dfrac{1}{1+y^2}=-\dfrac{1}{1+y^2}\)，对该函数有 \[ \forall \ \epsilon>0,\exist\ \delta=\epsilon>0,\forall \ \abs{y_1-y_2}<\delta,s.t.\abs{f(y_1)-f(y_2)}=\dfrac{\abs{(y_1-y_2)(y_1+y_2)}}{(1+y_1^2)(1+y_2^2)}\\ <\delta\cdot (\dfrac{\abs{y_1}}{(1+y_1^2)(1+y_2^2)}+\dfrac{\abs{y_2}}{(1+y_1^2)(1+y_2^2)})\leq\delta\cdot (\dfrac{1}{\abs{y_1+\dfrac{1}{y_1}}}+\dfrac{1}{\abs{y_2+\dfrac{1}{y_2}}})\leq\delta(\dfrac{1}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2})=\epsilon \] 从而 \(\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{y^2-x^2}{(x^2+y^2)^2}\) 对 \(y\in R\) 一致收敛

解：\(\displaystyle \int_0^{1}\ln (xy)dx\ (\dfrac{1}{2}<y<2)=x\cdot \ln(xy)\big |_0^1-\int_0^1x\cdot \dfrac{y}{xy}dx=\ln(y)-\lim\limits_{x\to 0}x\cdot \ln (xy)-1\)，其中 \[ \because y>0\ \therefore\lim\limits_{x\to 0}x\cdot \ln (xy)=\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\ln (xy)}{\dfrac{1}{x}}=\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{xy}\cdot y}{-\dfrac{1}{x^2}}=\lim\limits_{x\to 0}(-x)=0 \] 从而 \(f(y)=\displaystyle \int_0^{1}\ln (xy)dx\ (\dfrac{1}{2}<y<2)=\ln y-1\)，对该函数有 \[ \forall \ \epsilon>0,\exist\ \delta=\dfrac{1}{2}\epsilon>0,\forall \ y_1,y_2\in(\dfrac{1}{2},2)\ 且\ \abs{y_1-y_2}<\delta,s.t.\abs{f(y_1)-f(y_2)}=\abs{\ln(\dfrac{y_1}{y_2})}\\ \ln(\abs{1+\dfrac{y_1-y_2}{y_2}})\leq\ln(1+\dfrac{\delta}{y_2})<\ln(1+\dfrac{\delta}{\frac{1}{2}})<\ln(1+2\delta)<2\delta=\epsilon \] 从而 \(\displaystyle \int_0^{1}\ln (xy)dx\) 对 \(y\in(\dfrac{1}{2},2)\) 一致收敛

解： \(f(t)=\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{t}{x^3}e^{-\frac{t}{2x^2}}dx\ (t>0)=\int_1^{+\infin}e^{-\frac{t}{2x^2}}d(-\frac{t}{2x^2})=e^{-\frac{t}{2x^2}}\big |_1^{+\infin}=1-e^{-\frac{t}{2}}\)，该函数有 \[ \forall \ \epsilon>0,\exist\ \delta=2\epsilon>0,\forall \ \abs{t_1-t_2}<\delta,s.t.\abs{f(t_1)-f(t_2)}=\abs{e^{-\frac{t_1}{2}}-e^{-\frac{t_2}{2}}} \\=e^{-\frac{t_1}{2}}\abs{1-e^{\frac{1}{2}(t_1-t_2)}}=e^{-\frac{t_2}{2}}\abs{1-e^{\frac{1}{2}(t_2-t_1)}}\leq e^{-\frac{t_1+t_2}{2}}\abs{1-e^{-\frac{1}{2}\abs{t_1-t_2}}}\leq1\cdot(\dfrac{1}{2}\abs{t_1-t_2})<\epsilon \] 从而 \(f(t)=\displaystyle \int_1^{+\infin}\dfrac{t}{x^3}e^{-\frac{t}{2x^2}}dx\) 对 \(t>0\) 一致收敛
