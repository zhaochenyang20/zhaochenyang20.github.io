微积分WXF第五章作业 作者：鲁睿

习题5.1

4(2)

证明：由收敛数列一定有界有 \(\exist\ M>0,s.t.\forall \ n\in N^*,u_n<M\)，而 \(u_{2n+1}>0\)

则有 \(S_n=\displaystyle \sum_{i=1}^{n}u_{2i+1}\) 单调递增，且有 \(S_n<\displaystyle \sum_{i=3}^{n}u_i<M\) 由单调有界定理可得 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}u_{2n+1}\) 收敛

5

证明：原式可以等价变形消去相同项，并使用极限 \(\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}nu_n=\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}(n+1)u_{n+1}=0\) 得 \[ \displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(n+1)(u_{n+1}-u_n)=\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(i+1)(u_{i+1}-u_i)=\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^{n}((i+1)\cdot u_{i+1}-i\cdot u_{i}-u_{i})\\ \lim\limits_{n\to \infty}((n+1)\cdot u_{n+1}-u_1)-\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^{n}u_{i}=0-u_1-\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^{n}u_{i}=-u_1-\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}u_n \] 从而 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(n+1)(u_{n+1}-u_n)+\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}u_n=-u_1\) 为定值，两者收敛相互等价

6(1)

解：由等比数列求和公式 \(100(\dfrac{1}{4})^{n-1}=100\cdot \dfrac{1-\frac{1}{4^n}}{1-\frac{1}{4}}=\dfrac{400}{3}(1-(\dfrac{1}{4})^n)\)，由极限 \(\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(\dfrac{1}{4})^n=0\)

从而 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}100(\dfrac{1}{4})^{n-1}\) 收敛，收敛于 \(\dfrac{400}{3}\)

6(5)

解：将级数拆开有 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^nn^3}{2n^3+n}=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^n(n^3+\dfrac{1}{2}n-\dfrac{1}{2}n)}{2n^3+n}=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^n}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^n}{2n^2+1}\)

后者 \(\displaystyle {\dfrac{1}{2}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\abs{\dfrac{(-1)^n}{2n^2+1}}}<\dfrac{1}{4}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^2}\) 绝对收敛，则该级数收敛（柯西收敛准则）于 \(C\)

则 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^nn^3}{2n^3+n}=\dfrac{1}{2}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(-1)^n-C\)，而跳跃级数 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(-1)^n\) 不收敛，从而 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{(-1)^nn^3}{2n^3+n}\) 发散

6(7)

解：由三角函数恒等式 \(\arctan (n+1)-\arctan(n-1)=\arctan(\dfrac{(n+1)-(n-1)}{1+(n+1)\cdot (n-1)})=\arctan\dfrac{2}{n^2}\)

取 \(n=2k\) 有 \(\arctan(2k+1)-\arctan(2k-1)=\arctan(\dfrac{1}{2k^2})\)，则代入级数裂项相消得 \[ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\arctan(\dfrac{1}{2n^2})=\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^{n}\arctan(\dfrac{1}{2k^2})=\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\sum_{k=1}^{n}(\arctan(2k+1)-\arctan(2k-1))\\ =\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}(\arctan(2n+1)-\arctan1)=\dfrac{\pi}{2}-\dfrac{\pi}{4}=\dfrac{\pi}{4} \] 从而原级数 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\arctan(\dfrac{1}{2n^2})\) 收敛，收敛于 \(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\)

5.2

1(1)

解：由比较判别法，正项级数 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{(2n-1)2^{n-1}}<1+\sum_{n=2}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{2^{n-1}}\)，从而收敛

1(5)

解：当 \(n\to \infty\) 时，\((\dfrac{1+n^2}{1+n^3})^2\to \dfrac{1}{n^2}\)，有 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^2}\) 收敛，从而 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(\dfrac{1+n^2}{1+n^3})^2\) 收敛

1(7)

解：\(n\to \infty,\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\ln(\dfrac{n+1}{n-1})\to\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\cdot \dfrac{2}{n}\to n^{-\frac{3}{2}}\)，后者由积分收敛法收敛，则 \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\ln(\dfrac{n+1}{n-1})\) 收敛
